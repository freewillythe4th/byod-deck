Will MulhollandEducating the market

Adoption is an education problem. So I would teach the job, not announce the feature.

Diagnose first

Talk to non-adopters with the cohort. Is it the gate, discoverability, or skill. Most operators were never taught to sell direct, so teach the actual gap.

Teach in product

An empty-state lesson at the moment of intent, when they paste a sponsor email. Under ninety seconds, the outcome, not a feature tour.

Academy and proof

Short lessons: price it, kit it, pitch it, run it, prove it, plus templates that remove the blank page. The certified report becomes the case study that teaches the next.

They have a support page and an intro video. That is documentation. I have built the education layer above it before: eBay's Ads Academy lifted seller ad performance around 30% and moved adoption. Same engine, now teaching creators to sell direct.
